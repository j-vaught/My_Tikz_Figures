% Description: Positioning of writing theories along the writer--reader axis. Expressivism is emphatically writer-centered (writing as self-discovery), while McEnerney's Reader-Value Model is maximally reader-centered (``No one cares what is inside of your head''). Genre Studies and Activity Theory mediate between the poles by attending to community conventions and systems.
